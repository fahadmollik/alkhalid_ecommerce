What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

{{ site_name }} uses cookies to enhance your shopping experience and provide you with personalized services. Here's how we use different types of cookies:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation, shopping cart functionality, and secure access to your account.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously to improve our services.

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and settings to provide you with a personalized experience, such as language preferences and display options.

Shopping Cookies

These cookies remember items in your shopping cart, your browsing history, and help provide product recommendations based on your interests.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies.

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of certain cookies using industry opt-out tools for analytics and advertising cookies.

How to Disable Cookies in Your Browser

  1. Click on the three dots menu in the top right corner
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click on "Privacy and security"
  4. Click on "Cookies and other site data"
  5. Choose your preferred cookie settings

  1. Click on the menu button and select "Settings"
  2. Select the "Privacy & Security" panel
  3. In the "Cookies and Site Data" section, configure your preferences
  4. Choose "Standard", "Strict", or "Custom" protection
