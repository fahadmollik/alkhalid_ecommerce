Return & Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase

30-Day Return Policy

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to return any item for a full refund or exchange.

Return Conditions

Original Condition

Items must be in original, unused condition with all tags attached.

Original Packaging

Items must be returned in original packaging and containers.

Proof of Purchase

Valid receipt or order confirmation required for all returns.

Return Authorization

Contact customer service to obtain a return authorization number.

How to Return an Item

1

Contact Us

Email us or call our customer service team to initiate the return process.

2

Get Authorization

Receive your return authorization number and shipping instructions.

3

Package Item

Securely package the item in its original packaging with all accessories.

4

Ship Back

Send the package using the provided shipping label and tracking information.

Refund Processing

Processing Time

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your returned item.

Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Shipping Costs

Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Return Shipping

Customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective.

Non-Returnable Items

Please Note

The following items cannot be returned for health and safety reasons:

  • Perishable goods and food items
  • Personal care and hygiene products
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Digital downloads and software
  • Gift cards and promotional items

Need Help with a Return?

Our customer service team is here to assist you with your return or exchange.
